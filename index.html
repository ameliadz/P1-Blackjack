How to Play Blackjack

  • You will be dealt two cards initially. You may then choose to hit (be dealt another card) or stand (keep the cards you have).
  • Attempt to beat the dealer by getting a score as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.
  • It is up to each individual player if an ace is worth 1 or 11. Face cards are 10 and numbered cards are their face value.
  • There's money at stake for each round! Don't lose! Blackjack pays out 3:2, insurance pays 2:1.
  • You may also choose to double down on any round (double your bet and be dealt one final card), or take insurance (bet an additional half-bet that the dealer has Blackjack) if the dealer's upcard is an Ace.
